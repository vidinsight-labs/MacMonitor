**Öne çıkan özellikler:**
- Düz dilde sistem sağlık özeti ve akıllı öneriler

Verileriniz cihazınızda kalır; buluta gönderilmez.

## Açıklama (EN)

MacMonitor is a local system monitor for CPU, memory, disk, and security on your Mac.

**Highlights:**
- Plain-language health summary and smart local insights
- Menu bar CPU/RAM at a glance
- Load event timeline — "why did my Mac slow down?"
- Startup items security overview with change tracking
- macOS Widget and Shortcuts support
- Turkish and English UI

Your data stays on your device; nothing is sent to the cloud.
